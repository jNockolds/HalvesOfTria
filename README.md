# Halves Of Tria
Halves of Tria is set in [CityName], in the aftermath of a great catastrophe. Its inhabitants are trapped in a stasis caused by an imbalance of power, unable to truly die. 

You play as two characters simultaneously: Salt, the fallen deity of stability and the body, and Sulphur, the fallen deity of volatility and the soul. Explore the city and find your place in its ecosystem, all the while searching for your companion, Mercury, who dissapeared during the catastrophy.

## Key features
- Narrative
- Open-world enviroment
- Two player characters (controlled simultaneously)
- Unlockable and progressable abilities
- Simulated ecosystem of creatures and factions
- Procedural pixel-art animation
- Full controller support
